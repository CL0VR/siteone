ABOUT

John Patricks

John Patricks, or JP as we like to call him is the most experienced of our staff, he's a bit of a stiff at times but he more than makes up for it with his incredible feats. At the age of 15 he climbed Mt. Everest in under a week. He fought a polar bear naked while vacationing in Serbia, and most recently swam from New York to Spain (any insinuations that Mr. Patricks is dead are incredulous and laughable)

Pat Robinson

Pat Robinson was one of the lesser known children of the now infamous Swiss Family Robinson clan. Although never mentioned in the families countless journals she swears to God she did most of the work and single handedly save her entire ungrateful family.

Reggy "ACE" Osborn

Ace is by far our most enthusiastic team member and our very best driving coach! Always happy to help and with over 70yrs experience in the field of nature survival he was quite a catch. Great with kids and generally found playing hide-n-seek with JP, Ace is always ready to go whether it be four wheeling or digging to China.
